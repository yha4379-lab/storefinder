Privacy policy

How Stockist handles information.

This policy describes information processed when you browse the directory, change site preferences, submit a form, view comments, or interact with advertising.

Information you provide

The partnership and correction form requests a company or brand name, contact name, email address, and message. Form submissions are processed by Formspree and used to respond, evaluate suggestions, maintain records of corrections, and protect the form from abuse. Do not submit sensitive personal information.

Browser preferences and technical data

The site stores your light or dark theme choice in your browser’s local storage. This preference stays on your device unless you clear it. Our hosting providers may process standard request data such as IP address, browser type, requested page, timestamp, and security signals to deliver and protect the site.

Comments and third-party services

Comments are provided by Disqus. Opening a comment thread loads Disqus resources, and your interaction is governed by Disqus policies. Formspree processes contact submissions. Cloudflare hosts the production site. External store images are delivered by Unsplash.

Google advertising

Stockist uses Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve, personalize, and measure ads, subject to consent requirements and applicable law. You can learn about Google’s advertising data practices and manage ad personalization through Google’s ad settings. Ads do not influence editorial selection.

Your choices

You can block third-party cookies through your browser, avoid opening Disqus threads, clear local storage, or choose not to submit the contact form. Blocking some services may limit related features. To request access to or deletion of information submitted through our form, contact us using that same form and provide enough detail to locate the submission.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy as the site or its service providers change. Material revisions will be reflected by the date below. Privacy questions can be sent through the contact form.
