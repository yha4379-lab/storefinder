Editorial standards

Useful, independent, and clear about what we know.

These standards explain how Stockist selects stores, researches listings, labels verification, handles corrections, and separates editorial decisions from commercial activity.

Selection principles

A store may be considered when it offers a distinctive multi-brand selection, demonstrates meaningful expertise, supports independent design, or contributes to its local retail culture. Popularity alone is not enough, and absence from the directory is not a negative judgment.

We prioritize stores where a concise listing can provide value beyond a map result: what the shop is known for, the character of its assortment, and the types of labels a visitor may encounter.

Research and verification

Listings may draw on official store information, brand stockist directories, current retailer communications, and credible reports from the community. We compare sources where practical and avoid presenting uncertain claims as fact.

  • Store confirmed indicates information supported by the retailer or its official channels.
  • Admin verified indicates that the directory team reviewed reliable sources.
  • Community confirmed indicates a credible reader report reviewed against available evidence.
  • Recently reported identifies useful information that may still require stronger confirmation.

Brand information

“Known to carry” describes representative brand relationships, not a promise of current inventory. Seasonal buys, regional distribution, and product availability change without notice. Readers should contact the retailer before traveling specifically for a brand or item.

Corrections and updates

We welcome specific corrections with a source or first-hand explanation. Submissions are evaluated for reliability, relevance, and recency. Material errors should be corrected promptly; smaller changes are incorporated during routine reviews.

To submit a correction, use the contact form and identify the store, the detail that needs attention, and the best available supporting source.

Commercial independence

Advertising, sponsorship, affiliate relationships, or partnership discussions do not purchase editorial inclusion or favorable treatment. Sponsored material, if introduced, will be clearly labeled. The directory’s descriptions and selection decisions remain separate from advertising relationships.

Community comments

Store discussions are provided through Disqus so readers can share useful experiences and updates. Comments represent their authors, not Stockist. Spam, harassment, deceptive promotion, and irrelevant material are not welcome and may be moderated through the Disqus platform.
